Saptarshi Ghosh

Postdoctoral Researcher

Kavli Institute for Systems Neuroscience - NTNU

I am a postdoc with Prof. Yasser Roudi (SpiNor Group) at the Kavli Institute for Systems Neuroscience in Norwegian University of Science and Technology. I am currently working on the identification of Relevant Neurons , operating at different time scales in Big Neural Data as well as their role in the development of neural circuits in the Hippocampus. I am also interested in Spiking Neural networks and large-scale Neural dynamics.

Fresh from Oven
Machine Learning Assisted Chimera and Solitary States in Networks
Here, using supervised machine learning, we predict (a) the precise value of delay which is sufficient for engineering chimera and solitary states for a given set of system's parameters, as well as (b) the intensity of incoherence for such engineered states. Ergo, using few initial data points we generate a machine learning model which can then create a more refined phase plot as well as by including new parameter values.
